Morphology Features¶

  • Length - (um) skeleton length
  • Width - (um) average width of subsection in microns
  • head, midbody, tail
  • Area - (um^2) # of pixels within contour converted to um^2
  • Area/Length
  • Width/Length

Posture Features¶

  • Bends
  • Bend Count
  • Eccentricity
  • Amplitude
  • Wavelength
  • Track Length
  • Coils
  • Eigen Projections
  • Orientation

Motion Features¶

  • Velocity
  • Motion States
  • Crawling
  • Foraging
  • Turns

Path Features¶

  • Range
  • Dwelling
  • Curvature
